FAQ'S

What areas do you service?

We currently offer mobile detailing services across all of Queens, NY, and nearby areas of Long Island. Key service areas include Jamaica, Flushing, Astoria, Long Island City, Valley Stream, Elmont, Hempstead, Garden City, Mineola, Westbury, New Hyde Park, Franklin Square, Floral Park, and Great Neck.

How long does it take to details a car?

Depending on the service, a full detail can take 3-4 hours, while a premium wash typically takes 1-2 hours.

Do you have a mobile fee?

Typically, no mobile fee is charged unless we’re servicing distant locations.

Can you service a vehicle in an apartment complex?

Yes, as long as we are permitted to use the facilities at the location.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major forms of payment, including cash, credit cards, and digital payments. A 3.5% convenience fee may apply to credit card payments.
